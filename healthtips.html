🌿 Health Tips & Wellness Guide

Comprehensive health advice for a better lifestyle

💡 Daily Health Essentials

Stay Hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily to maintain proper hydration and support bodily functions.

Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support immune function and mental health.

Practice Good Hygiene

Wash hands frequently, especially before meals and after using the restroom.

Take Regular Breaks

Take short breaks every hour when working to reduce eye strain and improve productivity.

🥗

Nutrition & Diet

  • ✓
    Eat a Rainbow
    Include colorful fruits and vegetables in your diet for essential vitamins and antioxidants.
  • ✓
    Choose Whole Grains
    Opt for whole grains over refined grains for better fiber content and sustained energy.
  • ✓
    Limit Processed Foods
    Reduce intake of processed foods high in salt, sugar, and unhealthy fats.
  • ✓
    Practice Portion Control
    Use smaller plates and be mindful of portion sizes to maintain healthy weight.
  • ✓
    Include Protein
    Consume lean proteins like fish, chicken, beans, and legumes for muscle health.
🏃‍♂️

Physical Activity

  • ✓
    Aim for 150 Minutes Weekly
    Get at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week.
  • ✓
    Strength Training
    Include muscle-strengthening activities 2-3 times per week for bone health.
  • ✓
    Stay Active Throughout Day
    Take stairs instead of elevators, walk short distances, and stand while working.
  • ✓
    Find Activities You Enjoy
    Choose exercises you love - dancing, swimming, cycling, or team sports.
  • ✓
    Stretch Regularly
    Include stretching exercises to improve flexibility and prevent injuries.

🚨 Emergency Health Tips

Heart Attack Symptoms

Chest pain, shortness of breath, nausea, lightheadedness. Call emergency services immediately if you experience these symptoms.

Stroke Signs (FAST)

Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call emergency. Every minute counts in stroke treatment.

Severe Allergic Reaction

Difficulty breathing, swelling of face/throat, rapid heartbeat. Use epinephrine if prescribed and seek immediate medical help.

High Fever

Fever above 103°F (39.4°C) in adults or 100.4°F (38°C) in infants requires immediate medical attention.
